AEGIS PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: February 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Aegis collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your personal information when you visit our website, create an account, and take online assessments.

1. Information We Collect

  1. Account Data: Name, email address, password, phone number (optional), and other profile details.
  2. Assessment Data: Your exam responses, test scores, and any communication or chat logs during the exam session.
  3. Proctoring Data: With your consent, our Proctoring Provider may collect webcam video, audio, and desktop/screen activity during an exam session.
  4. Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and usage data such as the pages visited on our Site.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and Manage Services: Set up your account, schedule and administer tests, manage results, and communicate with you.
  • Proctoring: Verify exam integrity via AI-based or human-based proctoring. Any suspicious activity or recordings may be used to investigate potential cheating.
  • Improving Our Services: Analyzing user behavior, identifying technical issues, and refining exam content or platform features.
  • Marketing and Updates: With your consent, we may send newsletters or promotional emails. You can opt out via an unsubscribe link.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

  • Proctoring Providers: We share relevant user and exam data with our external proctoring partners to facilitate identity checks and exam monitoring. They are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Service Providers: Hosting, payment processors, analytics, and email service providers who assist us in operating our business.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose user data to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or court orders, or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Business Transfers: If Aegis is acquired, user data may be transferred to the new entity in compliance with applicable laws.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies or similar technologies to remember your preferences and understand how you use our Site. You can usually disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect certain features.

5. Data Retention

We store your personal information only as long as needed:

  • Exam Data is generally retained for 3 years for record-keeping or as required by partner institutions.
  • Proctoring Recordings are stored by the Proctoring Provider in compliance with our contracts. Typically retained for 30 days unless legal or investigative reasons require longer.

6. International Data Transfers

Your personal information may be stored and processed in countries outside your own. We use appropriate safeguards to ensure your data is protected consistent with this Policy.

7. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures: SSL encryption, restricted access, routine audits. However, no method of transmission online is 100% secure. You agree to notify us if you suspect any account breach.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent if processing is based on consent.
  • File a complaint with a regulatory authority if you believe your privacy rights are violated.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data from individuals under the age of 13 (or minimum legal age in your jurisdiction). If you believe we have unintentionally collected such data, please contact us to remove it.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may modify this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on our Site with the updated "Effective Date." Your continued use of Aegis after changes signifies acceptance of the updated Policy.
